# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Pinboard is an educational tool used in classrooms. We are committed to making this project a welcoming space for contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels — including beginners.

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in feedback
- Welcome newcomers and beginners warmly
- Give credit generously
- Focus criticism on ideas, not people
- Accept that maintainers make final calls on project direction

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or jokes
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional or educational setting

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, and any official STEM Sprouts communication channels.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to **conduct@stemsprouts.org**. All reports will be handled confidentially. Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who violate this code.
